# UAE Construction Regulations: Dubai Package (2025–2026)
**Electronic Regulatory Package for Engineers and Architects**

Welcome to the central repository for UAE and Dubai construction norms. This repository is designed for technical specialists to access up-to-date laws, codes, and guidelines efficiently.

### 🚀 Quick Navigation
1.  **[INDEX.md](./INDEX.md)** — Full technical list of all documents with direct links and metadata.
2.  **7 Categories** (Folders) — Organized PDF files (<25 MB).
3.  **Google Drive Backup** — For heavy PDF files (>25 MB) and high-res documents.

### 📂 Document Blocks
*   **01 Planning & Building** — The Dubai Building Code and fundamental civil laws.
*   **02 Energy Efficiency** — Al Sa'fat (Green Building) and energy saving norms.
*   **03 Permits & Completion** — Official procedures for DM permits and certificates.
*   **04 Zoning & Land Use** — Urban planning laws and DDA master planning.
*   **05 Architectural Profession** — Engineering consultancy laws and ethical codes.
*   **06 Fire & Life Safety** — Mandatory UAE Fire Code and cladding regulations.
*   **07 Licensing Firms** — Registration rules for contractors and consultants.

### 🛠 How to Use
- **PDF Links**: Most documents are available directly in this repo.
- **External Sources**: The `URL` field in `INDEX.md` points to the official government portal.
- **Transliterations**: Arabic titles are provided in parentheses for official reference.
